1. What Is Artificial Intelligence?
Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the science of creating machines that can think, learn, and make decisions like humans. AI systems analyze data, recognize patterns, and improve over time. AI is used in phones, education, healthcare, and business. Learning AI helps students prepare for future careers and solve real-world problems.
2. History of Artificial Intelligence
The history of AI began in the 1950s when scientists explored machine intelligence. Over decades, AI evolved from simple rule-based systems to advanced machine learning models. Today, AI powers smart assistants, recommendation systems, and automation.
3. How AI Works (Simple Explanation)
AI works by collecting data, processing it through algorithms, and learning from results. The more data AI systems receive, the better their performance becomes.
