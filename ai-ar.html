Artificial Intelligence & Augmented Reality

Learn how AI and AR are shaping education, business, and careers

What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the ability of machines and software to think, learn, and solve problems like humans.

  • AI can analyze data
  • AI can recognize images and speech
  • AI can help people make better decisions

What is Augmented Reality (AR)?

Augmented Reality (AR) adds digital objects, images, and information into the real world using phones, tablets, or computers.

  • AR shows 3D objects on real environments
  • AR is used in education, gaming, and training
  • AR helps students understand complex ideas visually

How Does AR Work?

  1. The device camera sees the real world
  2. Software detects surfaces or images
  3. Digital content is placed on top of reality
  4. The user interacts with the digital object
