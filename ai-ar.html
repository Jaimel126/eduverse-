Artificial Intelligence & Augmented Reality

Understand how AI and AR are transforming education, business, and careers

What is Artificial Intelligence (AI)?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the ability of computers and software to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence.

AI systems can learn from data, recognize patterns, make decisions, and continuously improve over time.

What is Augmented Reality (AR)?

Augmented Reality (AR) is a technology that places digital objects, images, or information into the real world using devices like phones, tablets, or computers.
